The best three movie according to Pooja

My top 3 movie names

Lagaan

This film highlights the cultural and patriotic spirit of India as a group of villagers challenge the British rulers to a cricket match to avoid paying unfair taxes.

Border

A gripping war drama that portrays the courage and sacrifice of Indian soldiers during the Indo-Pakistani War of 1971, showcasing the unwavering spirit of the Indian army.

Uri: The Surgical Strike

Based on true events, the film depicts the retaliatory surgical strike conducted by the Indian army on terrorist launch pads, showcasing the valor and determination of Indian soldiers.
